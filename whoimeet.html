Who I Would Like to Meet.

The person I would like to meet is Mikhail Gorbachev, the former president of the soviet union. Gorbachev was the person who made the choice to disband the soviet union. I would like to ask him why he chose to make such a difficult decision, and find out what was going on behind the scenes leading up to the fall of the USSR.

I would like to know what it was like being the leader of such a large country, and what his day to day life would be like. I would want to know how the country operated, his opinions on it, why he chose to live that life. I think it could give me some insight on how governments work, and especially Communist ones so different from our own. I would want to know what it was like with the US, and how he viewed us.
